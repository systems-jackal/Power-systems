Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my generator?

We recommend servicing every 6 months or after every 250 running hours, whichever comes first. Regular servicing ensures reliability and longevity.

What size generator do I need for my business?

It depends on your total load. Contact us for a free site assessment and load calculation – we'll recommend the perfect size.

Do you offer warranty on spare parts?

Yes, all our spare parts come with a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects. Labour on installations is also covered.

How does an ATS work?

An Automatic Transfer Switch monitors mains power and automatically starts the generator when an outage occurs, then transfers the load. When power returns, it transfers back and shuts down the generator.
